About Us

Established in 2020, MellowTech believe in creating Smart Homes with the relevant research & development experience and tech-centric approach.
Our philosophy is to bring innovation with the merger of the latest technology and create a skillful and sustainable environment for the country.

Our Vision

We intend to manufacture smart products with voguish technology which becomes a pillar to society. We yearn to follow a sustainable approach that delivers a better customer experience with end-to-end tech solutions.

Our Mission

Our quest is to become the most prominent electronics service provider that focuses on innovating products and automating homes with a touch of technology. We covet to ignite every home with our proficient mind.

Our Values

MellowTech is conceptualized by like-minded people who desire to grow their country in the electronic phase, adding more skillful people to their team and embracing the unique perspectives of the people they are surrounded by.
